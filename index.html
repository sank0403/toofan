Toofan!

How To Play
TOOFAN is a Test against time of your knowledge on the Indian Premiere League. The objective is to identify the mystery player, before the clock runs out.

You need to identify a player who has either been a Winning Captain, has won the Orange/Purple Cap, been the Man of the Match in the Finals, hit the Max no: of 6s, been the Player of the Series or been the Emerging Player of the series in a particular edition.

You get a total of 60 seconds to identify the player. At set intervals, you get a clue about the mystery player. You lose if the clock winds down!

Use the on-screen keyboard to key in your answer. You can have multiple goes and Hit Enter to submit. The clock is stopped when you identify the player successfully.

Pay attention to the number of characters in the players first and last names. This is another clue, that could be of help.

One of the below hints will be revealed at random after 5, 10, 20, 30, 40, and 50 seconds:-

  1. Year indicates the edition of the World Cup in which the player won the accolade.
  2. Country indicates the nationality of the player.
  3. Team indicates the team of the player, of which he was part of in that edition.
  4. Category indicates the actual accolade that the player achieved in that particular edition.
  5. FN FL indicates the first letter of the players first name.
  6. LN FL indicates the first letter of the players last name (middle name if applicable).
Legend : WC - Winning Captain | OC - Orange Cap | PC - Purple Cap | MF - MOM in the Finals | MVP - Player of the Series | FN FL - First Name First Letter | LN FL - Last Name First Letter | Max6s - Maximum 6s| EP - Emerging Player of the Series
